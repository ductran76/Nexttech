1. who we are

ShipChung .,JSC, one of PeaceSoft Group’s Companies, the first and only shipping gateway which connects online merchants to couriers. Founded in 2012 with purpose to solve the bottleneck of e-Commerce in Vietnam, after the first 2 years we have been succeeding partnership with 10 leading couriers as Vietnam Post, Viettel Post, Kerry Express, Netco, EMS, Nasco, etc with more than 50,000 registered accounts and approximately 120,000 orders per month. ShipChung simplifies shipping process for thousands of online merchants without contract. Clients also can track the journey of the consignment via SMS or directly in the website.
